How to Use
About

Choose the type of Exercise and duration, you can change these later via the settings menu.

Press start button to start, and immediately prepare yourself to do the Exercise.

Try to get optimal score (repetition) with proper technique and enjoy!

Body Weight Exercise

Body weight exercises are a type of strength training where the individual's own weight is used to provide resistance against gravity. These exercises are effective for building strength, endurance, flexibility, and balance without the need for external weights or machines. They can be performed anywhere and are often incorporated into fitness routines for their convenience and effectiveness.

How it Work?

mage data acquired from video or webcam is processed by a pose detector utilizing the MoveNet model to generate keypoints. These keypoints are used to calculate repetitions and serve as input for classifying exercise types with a Neural Network model.

Privacy Protection

The Pose Detector and Classification feature within the application functions entirely on the client side, guaranteeing that no image data is transmitted from the user's device. Any videos or images processed by the application are automatically deleted afterward.
